The data is copied from ["_Qing Mi Shu Wen_"](https://www.shidianguji.com/book/CADAL11202250/chapter/1lk09dczg8sun?page_from=searching_page&version=3) and ["_Qing Mi Shu Wen Xv_"](https://www.shidianguji.com/book/CADAL11202250/chapter/1lk09dczg8sun?page_from=searching_page&version=3). As the book has not yet been digitized, the data from "_Qing Mi Shu Wen Zai Xv_" has not yet been copied. 

Each row represents an entry in the book (one column or multiple columns). Variables such as official position, official, hometown, etc. were extracted from the "content" by DeepSeek.
